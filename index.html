 Sound Insulation

Noise pollution is major urban threat as noise enters from joints creacks in windows and joints from walls. Majority of sound insulation property depends upon on glass thickness and types, however windows also plays a important role in sound insulation. Thus drawing capability and fabrication quality in fusion welding parts should be well considered. uPVC products have been designed with multi-chambers which reduce sound upto 30-35 DB(decibels) to enjoy peace and quite at all times.

 Thermal Insulation

The PVC material itself is a poor conductor of heat or cold comparing to ordinary metal window. Thus UPVC windows with multi-chambered profiles will increase thermal efficiency that helps reduce the electrical bills drastically. This is why we call it as Energy saving eco-friendly products.
